PanExpedia Narrative Visualization for TOP 4 Most Visited Countries

Wonjong (Jeff) Shin

Extension from the PanExpedia's website and the dashboard, PanExpedia has published the Narrative Visualization regarding the COVID-19 situation comparison among the Top 4 SELECTED MUST-GO COUNTRIES!

For travelers in the world during COVID-19 pandemic, this is the COVID-19 situation comparisons are held in these criteria: daily positive COVID-19 test cases, daily COVID-19 vaccination rates, level of the medical systems among TOP 4 traveling countries! This project is intended to help people gain confidence in their trips as the pandemic goes on, giving them the power to decide for themselves whether or not to travel to a certain location. OWID COVID-19 Dataset is used for our project.

    Three Scenes of comparisons

  • Covid Cases
  • Covid Vaccination Rate
  • Medical Systems

Which country has higher number of Covid Cases?

If you want to unsee certain country's Covid cases, click the country in the legend section. Hover over the map to see the daily Covid Cases value

This chart element is very helpful to compare the trend of COVID-19 positive cases in each countries. By clicking each countries, you can focus on specific country's covid cases trend to create the conclusion regarding whether this country is dangerous or comparably less dangerous to travel.

Users can also see the certain number of the data when hover over the dot value. It shows the number of Covid cases in particular country on the n-th date of 2022.

Which country has higher COVID-19 Vaccination Rate?

Even the Covid Cases are bit high, the more vaccination would be also an indication of the safeness to travel in the certain country. Users can compare the tred of the vaccination rate and output the conclusion whether the destination country is safe to travel or not.

The y-axis value of this chart is a percentage of the vaccination rate based on the daily vaccination number and population. Typically most countries have a daily vaccinater rate between 1% ~ 2% of the population.

If the user want to check the country of the certain value of the graph, user can hover over the value point and it will indicate which country it is.

When getting hospitalized for COVID-19, which country's medical system is more likely to prevent from the death?

This scene is very important to the travelers all the time, but especially during the global pandemic. Users can check what percentage of the hospitalized patients passes away. This could be the indicator of how well the medical service in certain countries are managed during the global pandemic. This is highly important due to the increase number of hospitalized patients and decreasing of the available medical staff to take care fo the patients. PanExpedia team members do not want our users to get sick at the first point, but we also do not want users to get get enough medical care when they are in the sever condition.

The y-axis value of this chart is the (average of daily COVID-19 related hospitalization number) / (average of daily COVID-19 related death number). This would let users to conclude which country has more higher rate to die when hospitalized due to the shortage of medical support. Therefore, the higher in the hospitalization number and the lower in the death number, patients are likely to get sufficient medical support to prevent from the death.

The size of the bubble indicates the population, the bigger size of the bubble, the larger population number.

The color of the bubble indicates the continent that the country is located in.

If the user want to check which country the corresponding bubble is, then they could hover over the bubble and they would also see the population of the corresponding country and its death rate when hospitalized due to COVID-19.
